## Social Media

Plan and execute social media tasks using the social media manager. Request: $ARGUMENTS

### Agents Involved
- **social-media-manager** — Campaign planning, post creation, engagement analysis, community management
- **content-strategist** — Content alignment and brand voice consistency
- **performance-agent** — Metrics analysis and optimization recommendations

### Steps

1. **Understand the Request**
   - Parse the social media task type (campaign, posts, analytics, community)
   - Identify target platforms (LinkedIn, Twitter/X, Instagram, Facebook, YouTube)
   - Determine goals (brand awareness, engagement, lead generation, community growth)

2. **Campaign Planning** (if campaign task)
   - Define campaign objectives and key messages
   - Select target audience segments
   - Create a posting schedule with optimal timing per platform
   - Plan content mix (educational, promotional, engagement, user-generated)
   - Define success metrics and KPIs

3. **Post Creation** (if posts task)
   - Draft platform-specific copy respecting character limits and conventions
   - Suggest visual assets (image descriptions, video concepts, carousel layouts)
   - Include relevant hashtags and mentions
   - Write variations for A/B testing
   - Add clear calls to action appropriate to each platform

4. **Engagement Analytics** (if analytics task)
   - Review engagement metrics (likes, shares, comments, click-through rates)
   - Identify top-performing content and patterns
   - Analyze audience demographics and growth trends
   - Benchmark against industry standards
   - Recommend data-driven improvements to strategy

5. **Community Management** (if community task)
   - Draft response templates for common questions and feedback
   - Create escalation guidelines for negative sentiment or crises
   - Plan community engagement activities (polls, Q&As, challenges)
   - Define moderation policies and tone guidelines

6. **Deliver Results**
   - Compile deliverables in a structured format
   - Include platform-specific best practices and tips
   - Provide a timeline and next steps for execution
   - Save campaign plans or content calendars to the project

### Output
Social media deliverables matching the requested task type, including campaign plans, ready-to-publish posts, analytics reports, or community management playbooks.
