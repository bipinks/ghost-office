## Create Content

Plan and execute content tasks using the content strategist. Request: $ARGUMENTS

### Agents Involved
- **content-strategist** — Content planning, copywriting, SEO, and editorial strategy
- **product-manager** — Alignment with product goals and target audience
- **documentation-agent** — Technical accuracy review for developer-facing content

### Steps

1. **Understand the Request**
   - Parse the content task type (strategy, calendar, copy, SEO audit, blog post)
   - Identify the target audience and goals
   - Determine the platform or channel (website, blog, email, docs)

2. **Research and Plan**
   - Analyze existing content for gaps and opportunities
   - Identify relevant keywords and topics for SEO alignment
   - Review competitor content and industry trends
   - Define tone, style, and messaging guidelines

3. **Content Strategy** (if strategy or calendar task)
   - Define content pillars and themes
   - Map content to buyer journey stages (awareness, consideration, decision)
   - Create an editorial calendar with publishing cadence
   - Assign content types to channels (blog, social, email, docs)

4. **Content Creation** (if copywriting or blog task)
   - Draft content following brand voice and style guidelines
   - Optimize for SEO with target keywords, meta descriptions, and headings
   - Include clear calls to action
   - Structure content for readability (headings, lists, short paragraphs)

5. **SEO Audit** (if seo-audit task)
   - Analyze current content for keyword coverage
   - Check meta titles, descriptions, and heading structure
   - Identify missing alt text, broken links, and duplicate content
   - Review internal linking strategy
   - Generate a prioritized list of SEO improvements

6. **Review and Deliver**
   - Verify content accuracy and brand alignment
   - Check readability score and formatting
   - Provide revision suggestions and next steps
   - Save deliverables to the appropriate project location

### Output
Content deliverables matching the requested task type, including strategy documents, editorial calendars, polished copy, or SEO audit reports with actionable recommendations.
